Who We Are

"An artifact, or artefact (see American and British English spelling differences), is something made or given shape by humans, such as a tool or a work of art, especially an object of archaeological interest."
Wikipedia

Artifa is an online store that sells rare items. Our website is the destination for European collectors to buy and sell the world’s most important historic works of art, design, jewels.

We are a brand new store founded in 2020 but we are very sentimental and still live in the past missing all its treasure.

We understood the global reach online auction markets have. We also know that there are many people like us who wish to get back all those wonderful items we lost.

Whether you are looking for a unique or luxurious or precious artefact – if it exists in the world, it probably is for sale on Artifa. Our mission is to be the world’s favorite destination for discovering great value and unique selection.

We also give our client an opportunity to sell their own artifacts. If you have any unique item and you are bored with them, use our Store!
